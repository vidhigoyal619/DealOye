FAQs

  • How do I post an item for sale on DealOye?
    To post an item for sale, log in to your account, navigate to the "Products" section, and click on the "Post a Free Ad" button. Follow the provided steps to create your listing.
  • Is it free to use DealOye for buying and selling?

    Yes, DealOye is completely free to use for both buyers and sellers. There are no hidden fees or charges associated with using our platform.

  • Can I buy and sell academic materials on DealOye?

    Absolutely! DealOye is designed to facilitate the buying, selling, and exchange of various items, including academic materials, textbooks, and educational resources.

    DealOye provides various avenues for community engagement. You can participate in chat discussions, interact with peers, and explore mentorship opportunities. Join our community to foster connections, share knowledge, and make your college experience more enriching.

    Yes, we prioritize the security of our users. DealOye employs encryption protocols to ensure secure financial transactions, and we continually update our security measures to protect user data.
